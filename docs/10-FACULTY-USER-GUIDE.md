# Faculty User Guide - Final Year Portal

Welcome to the Final Year Project Portal! This guide will help you get started and understand how to use the system effectively.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started - First Time Login

### Step 1: Account Creation by Admin
**IMPORTANT:** Faculty accounts are created by the system administrator. You cannot self-register.

The admin will:
1. Create your account in the system
2. Use your **official institutional email address** (e.g., `faculty.name@gehu.ac.in`)
3. Set your role as "Faculty"

### Step 2: Your First Login

1. **Visit the Portal:** Navigate to the login page
2. **Click "Sign Up" or "Create Account":** Even though admin created your account in Firestore, you need to complete Firebase authentication setup
3. **Use Your Official Email:** 
   - ✅ Use the **exact email address** that the admin provided
   - ✅ Must be your institutional email (e.g., `@gehu.ac.in`)
   - ❌ Do NOT use personal emails (Gmail, Yahoo, etc.)
4. **Create a Strong Password:**
   - Minimum 6 characters (Firebase requirement)
   - Recommended: Use a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters
   - Example: `Faculty@2026!`
5. **Complete Registration:** Click the sign-up button
6. **Login:** After creating your password, use your email and password to log in

### Step 3: Verify Your Dashboard
After successful login, you should see:
- Your name and role displayed
- Faculty Dashboard with relevant sections
- Current academic session information

## 🎯 Portal Overview - How It Works

### Your Dual Role as Faculty

As a faculty member, you have **two important roles**:

#### 1. **Mentor Role**
- Guide and supervise student teams throughout their project journey
- Review and approve project proposals
- Monitor team progress
- Provide technical guidance

#### 2. **Panel Evaluator Role**
- Evaluate teams during different project phases
- Provide grades and feedback
- Participate in evaluation panels
- **IMPORTANT:** You cannot evaluate teams you mentor (system enforces this)

---

## 📊 Main Features & Workflows

### 1. **Mentorship Management**

#### Receiving Mentorship Requests
1. **Notification:** You'll receive a notification when a team requests you as a mentor
2. **Review Request:** Go to "Mentorship Requests" section
3. **View Details:** See team information, project title, and team members
4. **Decision:** Accept or decline the request
   - ✅ **Accept:** You become their mentor and appear in their dashboard
   - ❌ **Decline:** Team can request another faculty member

#### Managing Your Teams
- **Dashboard View:** See all teams you mentor
- **Project Progress:** Track each team's phase status
- **Submissions:** Review team submissions for each phase
- **Communication:** View and respond to team updates

### 2. **Panel Evaluations**

#### How Panel Assignments Work
- Admin assigns you to evaluation panels for different phases
- Each panel evaluates multiple teams
- **Conflict Prevention:** You won't be assigned to evaluate teams you mentor

#### Evaluation Process
1. **Access Panel:** Go to "Panel Evaluations" section
2. **Select Phase:** Choose the active evaluation phase (Synopsis, Phase 1, Phase 2, Final)
3. **Select Team:** Pick a team from your assigned panel
4. **Evaluate:** Fill out the evaluation form with:
   - Grades for different criteria
   - Written feedback
   - Comments and suggestions
5. **Submit:** Save your evaluation

#### Editing Evaluations
- You can edit evaluations **only during the active phase**
- Once a phase deadline passes, evaluations become read-only
- This ensures fairness and prevents retrospective changes

### 3. **Viewing Team Progress**

**Team Information:**
- Project title and description
- Team members and their roles
- Current phase status
- Submission history

**Submission Review:**
- View documents submitted by teams
- Check submission timestamps
- See version history
- Provide feedback

## 📅 Academic Sessions & Phases

### Understanding Sessions
- Each academic year is a separate "session" (e.g., 2025-2026)
- All teams, evaluations, and data are session-specific
- Admin manages session transitions

### Project Phases
Teams progress through sequential phases:

1. **Synopsis Phase**
   - Initial project proposal
   - Team formation
   - Mentor assignment

2. **Phase 1 - Mid-term Review**
   - Progress presentation
   - Initial implementation
   - Mid-term evaluation

3. **Phase 2 - Pre-final Review**
   - Major implementation milestone
   - Testing and refinement
   - Pre-final evaluation

4. **Final Phase - Internal Evaluation**
   - Complete project demonstration
   - Final documentation
   - Internal panel evaluation

5. **External Phase**
   - External evaluator assessment
   - Final grades
   - Project completion

Each phase has:
- **Start Date:** When the phase becomes active
- **Deadline:** Last date for submissions
- **Evaluation Period:** Window for panel evaluations

## ✅ Best Practices

### As a Mentor
1. **Respond Promptly:** Accept/decline mentorship requests quickly
2. **Regular Check-ins:** Monitor your teams' progress regularly
3. **Timely Feedback:** Review submissions before phase deadlines
4. **Clear Communication:** Provide actionable feedback
5. **Be Available:** Support teams during critical phases

### As a Panel Evaluator
1. **Fair Evaluation:** Use consistent criteria across all teams
2. **Detailed Feedback:** Write constructive comments
3. **Meet Deadlines:** Complete evaluations before phase ends
4. **Conflict Awareness:** Report if you accidentally see a team you mentor in your panel
5. **Objective Assessment:** Evaluate based on merit, not personal bias

### General Tips
1. **Use Official Email:** Always login with your institutional email
2. **Secure Password:** Choose a strong, unique password
3. **Regular Login:** Check the portal at least 2-3 times per week
4. **Read Notifications:** Don't miss important updates
5. **Contact Admin:** If you face issues, contact the system administrator

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Common Issues & Solutions

#### "Email already in use"
- **Cause:** You're trying to sign up but already have an account
- **Solution:** Use "Login" instead of "Sign Up"

#### "User not found" or "Access Denied"
- **Cause:** Admin hasn't created your account yet
- **Solution:** Contact admin to verify your account is created with correct email

#### Can't see any teams
- **Cause:** No teams have requested you as mentor, or admin hasn't assigned you to a panel
- **Solution:** Wait for student requests or contact admin for panel assignments

#### Can't evaluate a team
- **Possible Reasons:**
  - You're the team's mentor (system prevents this)
  - Phase is not active yet
  - Phase deadline has passed
- **Solution:** Check phase dates and your role for that team

#### Forgot Password
- **Solution:** Use the "Forgot Password" link on login page
- Enter your institutional email to receive reset instructions
